Sophomore Year

Currently, I am still in the proccess of taking my Sophomore Year. However, I still would like to share my experience so far as the experience in my Sophomore year so far has differed a little from my freshman year. First off, the course difficulty struck up another level, I am currently struggling in Calculus II but I it looks like I will pull through in the end. However, the computer science courses are what surprised me the most. Currently, I am taking Computer Architeciture which is a very theoretical heavy course which is not one of my strong suits.

So, my advice so far to Computer Science students is too never take a Computer Science course lightly even if you are confident in your coding abilities. That is what happened to me. Although I am stil doing fine when it comes to grades, I definitely could have done better if I had focused and dedicated more time to these courses at the beginning of the semester.
