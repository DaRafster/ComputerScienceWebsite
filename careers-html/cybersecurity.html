Cyber Security

Security in the online world will always be a priority as a means keep billion of people's online information safe. Cyber-attacks and security threats tend to get increasingly more common as technology continues to evolve, so the demand for cyber security will always be high due to its importance. Working in cyber security as a junior can get you $60,000 - $70,000 a year. The more experience you get, the salary can increase up to the $90,000 / year range.

In cyber security you will be in charge of defending your company or client's safety against online threats. Apart of this job requires you to stay up to date with the latest technology. This means you have to be constantly learning about everything being introduced into the technological world and determine if they are a potential threat that needs precautions against. The responsibility of preventing information breaches lie within your hands which is why this is such a highly respected and well paying job.
