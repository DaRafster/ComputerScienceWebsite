Game Development

Game Development is a unique career path for most Computer Science students. Although the prospect of making games sounds fun, the work required in the career is quite a lot and can be stressful due to strict deadlines. However, if you do want to enter into the game development field, do not be discouraged. In Game Development, although the salaries vary, you can still make a solid salary depending on your company and your role in the creation of the game.

As for your responsiblities in game development, again, they vary on your position. In game development, you coudl be reponsible for translating ideas into the code itself, handling the audio or gameplay mechanics, responsible for creating the game environment etc. There is a wide list of potential reponsibilties a game programmer could have. If you are interested in pursuing game development, there most likely is a role that fits your capabilitiesdue to the wide number of positions available in the field.
