Data Science

Data Science is known to be one of the most promising jobs worldwide. Although, data science is also known to be one of the hardest career paths to get into. Obtaning a job in data science usually requires an "x" amount of years of experience in the field. Usually you would start out in a different occupation and slowly work towards your way towards a data science position. However, if you do land a job, you will be set in the long-term. The average salary of a data scientist starts at $90,000 and goes up from there with experience.

The responsibilities of a data science consists of collecting data, analyzing data, and creating real-world solutions based on data obtained. Although it sounds like as a data scientist you will not need any programming knowledge, this is actually completely false. Knowing programming languages is exteremely important for data scientist to create algorithms that will allow them to solve modern problems. If you are interested in the career choice of data science, it will be difficult to get into the field but will be very rewarding when achieved.
