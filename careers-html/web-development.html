Web Development

Web Development is another solid career choice. Web Development can be a very fun career path as your given the flexbility and freedom to be creative. The career path is in demand and will continue to be in demand for the coming years. Many companies strive for high quality websites as a means to reach out to their customers, and in order to achieve this they require web developers. As a web developer, your salary varies based on your reputation and quality of your work. In Canada, the average salary of a web developer falls within the range of $60,000 - $70,000 / year and increases the more experience and better reputation you have as a web developer.

One notable aspect of web development which many may struggle with is the competition. Getting a job in the world of web development is no easy feat. In addition, you will be given strict deadlines and specific requests by your clients and it will be your reponsbility to live up to their expectations and requirements. Web development is a highly respected career choice, and if this sounds like a career you would like to get into, I highly encourage you give it a shot.
